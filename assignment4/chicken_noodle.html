Farm-Style Chicken Noodle

Classic, comforting, a cure-all for whatever ails you, this soup is just like grandma use to make. This easy recipe uses a rotisserie chicken from your local market, fresh carrots and celery, a sweet Vidalia onion and egg noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 medium Vidalia sweet onion
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano
  • 12 ounces egg noodles
  • 3 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat your choice of a stock in a pot on medium high heat on our stove.
  2. Add oil. Once oil is hot, add carrots, onion, and celery cook until the onions are translucent and the carrots are tender. Add garlic and cook for one minute more.
  3. Add fresh thyme and oregano until aromatic.
  4. Add chicken broth and bring to boil. Boil gently for 5 more minutes.
  5. Add noodles and cook until noodles are desired doneness.
  6. Add shredded chicken and let simmer until chicken is thoroughly heated. Salt and pepper to your liking.
