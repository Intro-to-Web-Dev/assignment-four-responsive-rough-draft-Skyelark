Cheesy Broccoli Cheddar

Creamy cheesy broccoli soup broccoli cheddar soup packed with carrots, broccoli, garlic, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter ½ stick
  • ½ medium onion chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic minced
  • 4 tablespoon AP flour
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 3 cups broccoli florets or 1 large head, cut into small pieces
  • 1 large carrot grated, julienned or finely chopped
  • 2 cups half & half or milk or light or heavy cream
  • 8 ounces block grated sharp cheddar cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large stock pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook 3-4 minutes or until softened and light gold.
  3. Add the garlic and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add flour and whisk for 1-2 minutes or until the flour begins to turn golden in color.
  5. Pour in the chicken stock, broccoli florets, carrots, and seasoning. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 15 minutes or until the broccoli and carrots are cooked through.
  6. Stir in half & half and cheddar cheese and simmer for another minute.
