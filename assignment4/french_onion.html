Savory French Onion

A simple French onion soup recipe full of the richest caramelized onion, crisp sherry wine, and fresh herb flavors.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup of butter
  • 6 large Vidalia onions, julienned
  • 2 tbsp of fresh thyme
  • 2 tbsp of fresh rosemary
  • 1 cup of dry sherry
  • 6 cups beef broth
  • 1 French baguette
  • 2 cups of gruyere cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a stock pot on medium heat. Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  2. Add fresh herbs and cook until aromatic.
  3. Add sherry to pot and reduce by half.
  4. Add beef broth and simmer soup for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Preheat the oven to a high setting broiler.
  6. Pour soup into broil safe bowls. Add 1 slice of baguette and top with cheese.
  7. Broil for 3-5 minutes until cheesy is melted and beginning to brown.
